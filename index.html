What We Do

Four core pillars drive everything we do at Gamuna Foundation.

🎓

Youth Empowerment

Training, workshops, and mentorship programs that build leadership, entrepreneurship, and personal development skills.

📢

Advocacy

We advocate for the rights and needs of young people in Papua New Guinea, giving them a voice in local and national discussions.

✨

Motivation & Inspiration

Seminars, outreach programs, and platforms for youth to share their stories and inspire one another toward better futures.

🌱

Community Development

Community service projects, job partnerships, and internships that promote social responsibility and environmental sustainability.

About Gamuna Foundation

Est. 2026 – Born in Madang, Built for Papua New Guinea's Future

Our Vision

To create a vibrant and empowered generation of young leaders and communities who contribute positively to society, fostering sustainable development and community resilience in Madang and in Papua New Guinea.

Our Mission

To empower young people through education, advocacy, and community engagement, enabling them to overcome challenges and achieve their full potential.
